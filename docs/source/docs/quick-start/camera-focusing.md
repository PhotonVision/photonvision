# Camera Focusing

## Prepare Camera

To ensure that your camera is focused properly, mount it to a secure surface and ensure it does not move darasticly. Point your camera at a detailed surface like a calibration board, this may help with focusing.

## Using Focus Tool
:::{important}
The focus tool will assign a "Score" to the current focus, this is score depends on your enviorment and the lighting, and cannot be used for comparison
:::
- In the Cameras tab, turn on focus mode
- Turn the lens on your camera to try and get the focus value as high as possible
- Once you cannot get a higher value, this indicated that your camera is fully focused and can be set it place using glue if desired.
